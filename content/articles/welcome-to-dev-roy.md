# Hello, World!

Welcome to **Dev-Roy**. This blog is designed to be minimal, elegant, and fast.

## Why this blog?
I wanted a space to share my thoughts on Technology, IT Infrastructure, Networking, Cybersecurity, and more, without the distractions of a bloated website.

- **Minimalist Design**: Inspired by Apple's clean aesthetics.
- **Fast Performance**: Built with Next.js and statically exported.
- **Easy Management**: Powered by Decap CMS via GitHub.

Enjoy reading!
